DESIGN

Hi, I’m Katie, a student designer and developer focused on digital media. I create engaging visuals and interactive content to help brands connect with their audience and stand out. My goal is to combine creativity and technology to deliver impactful digital experiences.

Currently studying Web Design and Development, I’ve developed skills in Adobe Creative Suite and coding languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My goal is to work in Web Design, Multimedia Design, and Branding to help create impactful digital experiences. Outside of school, I enjoy thrifting, watching shows and movies, reading, trying new restraunts and spending time with my 2 year old Tortoiseshell kitty.
