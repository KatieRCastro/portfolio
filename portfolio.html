DESIGN

BOOK JACKET MEMOIR

The purpose of the book jacket design was to entice a targeted audience to explore a true story about my life while showcasing my personal aesthetic and creative abilities. The project required a balance between personal storytelling and professional presentation, ensuring that the design appealed to both potential publishing houses and specific readers with interests aligned to the themes of my memoir. By investigating the conventions of book jacket design, such as typography, layout, and imagery, I aimed to create a visually compelling and genre-appropriate design that could effectively capture the attention of my intended audience.

IMAGE TYPE LAYOUT

This assignment of experimenting with type, layout, and image to create unorthodox designs played a crucial role in developing my creativity and problem-solving abilities as a designer. By stepping away from traditional design conventions, this exercise encouraged me to push boundaries and think outside the box. EVENT POSTER

The purpose of this assignment was to create a visually compelling event poster tailored to a specific audience, purpose, and context. LOGOS

The purpose of this assignment was to design a logo that effectively represents a brand or organization while appealing to a specific audience and fulfilling a clear purpose. Through this project, I learned how to analyze a brand's identity, values, and target audience to create a design that communicates its essence. NON PROFIT BRANDING

The purpose of the De’ Medici Foundation’s logo was to represent the foundation’s mission and history in a clear and meaningful way. The design needed to show their commitment to helping others while honoring the artistic legacy of the Medici family. The dove was chosen to symbolize peace, hope, and renewal, reflecting the foundation’s work with disadvantaged children and global causes. The stained glass element connects to the Medici family's role as patrons of Renaissance art, representing creativity, culture, and beauty. The design process focused on combining these elements to create a logo that feels both modern and timeless. The final design captures the foundation’s dedication to both humanitarian efforts and the arts.

DIGITAL BOOK

The purpose of this project was to design a 10-page full-color digital booklet that balances aesthetics with functionality while keeping the reader engaged. This assignment introduced the challenges of multi-page design, such as creating a cohesive layout, organizing content effectively, and maintaining consistency throughout. The booklet needed to include essential elements like a front cover, table of contents, body content, and back matter, all designed to aid readability and memory.

DATABASE

SCHOOLS DATABASE

In this assignment, I created a spreadsheet to track and project school enrollment over several years. I used formulas to calculate each year's enrollment based on a set percentage increase. I also totaled the enrollment for each year and created bar charts to show the trends. I made a summary sheet to display total enrollment by school type and included a bar chart for comparison. The goal was to organize and visualize the enrollment data over time.

AUTORAMA SAFETY PRODUCTS DATABASE

In this assignment, I used Google Sheets to organize and analyze product data for "South University Autorama Safety Products." I formatted the sheet to highlight key information, applied necessary calculations for retail values and percentages, and ensured all data was clearly presented. I then created a pie chart to visualize the percentage breakdown of total retail value. The goal was to organize the data, perform the necessary calculations, and present the results in an easy-to-understand format.
